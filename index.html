Working Experience

Even before my Masters Degree's graduation in October, I have started to look for my first job. I still remember the excitement in preparing my resume, bringing out my creativity to the max to come out with a decent template and filled it up with all the related information. I have sent out my resume with various job applications with satisfaction. I can recall that I applied for various position such as Business Analyst, Management Trainee for a few banks, Equity Analyst, Wealth Management Associate and Customer Services. However, there was totally no reply from any of the job applications, even though I have called to follow up. Fortunately, after 3 months of trying and waiting, I was able to land a job in OCBC Bank Sdn Bhd as part of the trade product management team in 2018.

  • These are my jobscopes as a member in trade product management team :
    • Review trade products in annual basis unless a drastic change in features
    • Monthly reporting to the senior management for monthly planning
    • Data analysis on customer profiles from the reports prepared
    • Plan, prepare and conduct campaigns for selected cluster of customers to maintain customer relationship
    • Prepare memo for strategic adjustment related to businesses

I am really grateful as I was given a chance to learn something new in the team. It was a fruitful one since I joined the bank. Really appreciate the care and patience that my colleagues have given to me while guiding me throughout the days in the bank. However, I have never give up in pursing my goal that I want to be in part of the investment banking team, treasury team or equity research team. I believe that I am ready to take on hurdles and challenges with my passion and knowledge. Thus, I would like to take one step forward in pursing my next career goal. Everyone has to start somewhere to become an expert in a specific field. Don't you agree?
